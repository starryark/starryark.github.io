I graduated with a **B.S. in Molecular and Cell Biology** from **UC San Diego, Class of '26**. After a lot of reflection, I decided I have little interest in following the conventional path ahead. Instead, I want to spend my time on things I find genuinely meaningful — work that adds value in an instrumental sense, but also in a Kantian one.

---

### What I'm Into

**Artificial Intelligence** — I believe technology will reshape how humanity operates, starting with the generation growing up inside it. I want to be part of what I consider the productive side of that shift, not the noise around it.

**Art** — Particularly the illustrative end of anime. I drift between thinking I draw well and thinking the opposite. Visual novels occupy a strange and underappreciated space — I sometimes think of them as the Shakespearean play of modernity.

**Crows** — I feed crows on campus. Watching them helps me step outside the anthropocentric framing that fills most of daily life.
